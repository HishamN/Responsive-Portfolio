About Me

My name is Hisham Noman and I go by Sean. I have a degree on business administration from Georgia State University. So for the past 14 years I have been an entrepreneur and I was successful on most of the businesses I got in to. I was living in Atlanta Georgia for more than 16 years and I just moved to Orlando Florida on 2016 to continue my journey as an entrepreneur and started a restaurant with my wife. I always loved the web and coding, so I started to teach my self and took some courses online and watched many videos to learn HTML and CSS but I always believed that I can change my career and become a successful web developer if I find the right source such as UCF Coding Boot Camp, I’ve focused my energies on the web, and I am happy that I joined UCF Coding Boot Camp it is a wonderful decision.

Since I started the Coding Boot camp I now spend my whole day, practically every day, experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and inhaling a wide variety of potentially useful information. I’m curious, and I enjoy work that challenges me to learn something new and stretch in a different direction.
